Rules/Player Expectations

These are the rules/expectations and also my style as a GM (if you're a new player feel free ask me questions if you don't understand some things)

• Fudging rolls - I will be rolling in front of the players majority of the time. I prefer this because while I understand fudging rolls to make the experience better, but this campaign setting is supposed to be especially gritty.

• Racial stuff - This doesn't mean races in D&D (I get that there's racial seggregation in-game e.g. Elves dislike dwarves, but I feel like that's a LOT different), I will not be allowing racist crap and slurs and all that for obvious reasons so I will not hesistate to kick you for that.

• Sexist stuff - Basically the same for this as racism, it's very obviouslly wrong so rape and stuff like that is not allowed and you'll be kicked on my call.

• PvP- I will highly discourage pvp even though I do allow evil alignments. I think that betrayal is interesting and would allow it if you're willing to give up YOUR character. Disputes should be handled in roleplay in terms of combat AND verbal tension (e.g. rolling for persuasion.)

• Attention - It takes a good amount of time to prepare this stuff and doing something (e.g. playing a game) while were in the game is pretty annoying. I'll do my part to make the world as immersive as possible so you should do yours, or what's the point?

• Disruptions - Another immersion breaking thing is blurting out or talking over me. There's really nothing worse as a player or GM when someones having an epic moment and some babbling idiot yells a shitty joke. I hate feeling like a freaking teacher so if you're putting me in that situation time and time again, I'm kicking you.

• Rule Disputes - This can really mess up the pace of the game, so if nobody knows the rule and it takes too long for me to find it, I'll just write down a note and check on it after the session and correct it later. I think following the rules is important but it's a game and having fun is a trillion times more important.

If you have any issues or possibly want something added let me know.
