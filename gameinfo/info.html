The Dark Realm of Dreigoth Wiki

Explanation

This is a west marches style campaign. Basically what that means is instead of a huge overarcing story there's one huge overarcing world (not to say there wont be anything like building up to bossfights or anything like that). Players will all be part of an adventurer's guild where they will go on quests all over Dreigoth. There'll be a group of players and you'll decide on a quest, who's going, and when you'll be doing it together. Usually a quest is about a session long (4-6 hours) but some may take longer than that.

Your character will slowly grow and possibly even achieve their goals while exploring the many realms of Dreigoth. Each party of usually up to 6 players will work together to take notes. It's pretty important that this is done. Locations and notable people will be put on the wiki as well as information about the world in general such as religion and history. For instructions on using the wiki go here. Be warned though, Dreigoth isn't called the DARK REALM for nothing, it's a dangerous place and adventuring is considered by lots to be just shy of suicide.
